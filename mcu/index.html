Our Goals

Bring more interaction within the MCU/Mineplex community.

We strive to improve and form communication within the Mineplex community. We encourage members to participate in events, and talk to people to hopefully make new friends. Since the very beginning of this team, our goal has been to form a family-like bond between the diversity of Mineplex.

Unite the community.

Working to eliminate disconnection between the MCU community, Mineplex community, and the Mineplex staff team has been a goal we try to achieve on a daily basis. We want to provide a safe and positive space for all players across all platforms. We hold public events, post threads, speak through our MCU staff members to the staff team, compete in competitive events, and interact in the Mineplex Community Council (MCC) on a regular basis to help achieve this goal.
